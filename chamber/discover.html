Bulawayo Demographics

The city of Kings -Bulawayo- is the second largest city in Zimbabwe. People here speak an nice language called "Ndebele" or rather "Isindebele".
The city is located south-west of the country and it is its own province.
According to Wikipedia:
The city's population is disputed; the 2012 census listed it at 653,337, while the Bulawayo City Council claimed it to be about 1.2 million. Bulawayo covers an area of about 1,707 square kilometres (659 square miles) in the western part of the country, along the Matsheumhlope River. Along with the capital Harare, Bulawayo is one of two cities in Zimbabwe that is also a province.
